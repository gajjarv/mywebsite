High-time resolution full polarization capabilities for the BL backend at GBT

Robert C. Byrd Green Bank Telescope (GBT) is world's largest single dish radio telescope. The Breakthrough Listen team have deployed a state-of-the-art highly-flexible 64-node GPU-equipped compute cluster. 
I worked with a team of engineers and carried out a number of commissioning observations to enable extremely high-time resolution and full polarization capabilities. These commissioning test resulted in one of the very first detection of FRB at the highest radio frequency of 8 GHz. 

Li & Gajjar et al. 2019
